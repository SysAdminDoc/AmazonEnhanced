Tools & data

Privacy

AmazonEnhanced stores settings, price history, country-of-origin cache, watched-order dates, custom brand rules, and allergen terms locally in this browser profile. The current release sends no analytics, telemetry, or shopping data to external services.

Accessibility & safety

Theme & density

Tile wraps each image in a soft card (default, no color distortion). Dim softens via brightness/contrast. Darken reduces opacity to 75% (inspired by stonecrusher's userstyle). Invert flips every image. Smart auto-inverts only white-bg images via canvas sampling.
