Theme & density

Amazon product images are white-background JPGs that look harsh on dark themes. Tile (default) wraps each in a soft light card — no color distortion. Dim softens without inverting. Invert flips every image (wrecks photos, great for icons/text). Smart samples corner pixels to detect white backgrounds and inverts only those; may fall back to tile if Amazon's CDN blocks canvas reads.
